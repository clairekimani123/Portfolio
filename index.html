About Me

I'm a full-stack software developer with a passion for building applications that are not just functional, but genuinely delightful to use. I graduated from Moringa School's Software Engineering program and I have finished my attachment as a Backend Developer Intern at Content Aggregation Limited in Nairobi.

My journey into tech started with curiosity and a drive to solve real problems. I've built and deployed production applications ranging from NGO fundraising platforms to AI-powered job trackers — each one teaching me something new about clean code, good architecture, and user-centred design.

I'm comfortable across the full stack — from React frontends and TailwindCSS interfaces to Flask APIs, PostgreSQL databases, and cloud deployments. I'm currently deepening my expertise in TypeScript and cloud infrastructure.

When I'm not coding, I'm learning — whether that's a new framework, a new tool, or a new concept. I believe the best developers never stop being students.
